Like Learning & Design?

Make a difference on campus and learn new skills as a Student Design and User Experience (UX) Assistant with UC San Diego Library!

We're looking for creative and collaborative student employees to join our Digital Experience team. In this role, we'll help you:

  • get hands-on experience assisting with UX research projects;
  • learn inclusive approaches to designing for diverse campus communities;
  • build your portfolio and gain new skills in team-based rapid prototyping, UX design, and content creation.

This job is all about learning, so you don't need to have extensive previous experience with design and technology tools. We'll teach you everything you need to know on the job! Just bring lots of curiosity and a passion for helping others!

What's UX?

User Experience (UX) is a growing field that focuses on building a deep and authentic understanding of people and their experiences in order to truly center diverse communities of users in the design process.
